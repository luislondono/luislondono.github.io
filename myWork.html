Computer Science Major (CS)

Requirements for Major Affiliation: Computer Science

At least C (not C–) in all completed CS and math courses. GPA ≥2.5 in CS 2110 (or CS 2112) and 2800. GPA ≥2.5 in MATH 1920 and CS 2800. Qualifying courses must be taken at Cornell for a letter grade. Grades in critical math and CS courses may be considered to offset deficiencies in meeting the GPA criteria. Visit the CS undergraduate website to learn more about alternative criteria for affiliation: www.cs.cornell.edu/undergrad/uadmis/becoming-cs-major
